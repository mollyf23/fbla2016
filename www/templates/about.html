Summary

This project is a mobile application that implements a digital yard sale to raise funds to attend the Nation Leadership Conference (NLC) for the Future Business Leaders of America.

Items can be viewed and donated using desktop, or mobile (IOS or Andriod). The donated items includes a picture, price, rating, and summary information. Users are able to browse the items, donate items, and comment on each items page. This application is a proof of concept.
